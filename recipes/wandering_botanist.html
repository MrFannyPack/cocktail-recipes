Wandering Botanist

Description:

Spirit forward? More like knock you on your ass.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Botanist gin
  • 1.5 oz compari
  • 1.5 oz dry vermouth
  • 0.5 oz lillet blanc
  • Citrus peel

Steps

  1. Mix gin, compari, vermouth, and lillet blanc in a shaker
  2. Shake it like a polaroid picture
  3. Garnish with citrus peel
