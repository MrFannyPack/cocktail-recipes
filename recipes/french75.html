French 75

Description:

Champers? Gin? In a coupe? Fancy AF.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz gin
  • 1 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 3 dashes simple syrup
  • 4 oz Champagne
  • Garnish: Lemon Slice

Steps

  1. Mix gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a shaker
  2. Shake what yo mama gave you
  3. Garnish with lemon slice
