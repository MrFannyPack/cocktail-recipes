Parrot Pact

Description:

This drink be slappin frfr.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz gin
  • 1/2 oz ginger syrup
  • 1/4 oz honey syrup
  • 3/4 oz lemon juice
  • Top with grapefruit raddler

Steps

  1. Mix gin, ginger syrup, honey syrup, and lemon juice in a shaker
  2. Shake it like a polaroid picture
  3. Top with grapefruit raddler
